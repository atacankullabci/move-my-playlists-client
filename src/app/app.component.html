Im Movin' App needs a permission from your spotify account to modify your library. Im Movin' app helps you to migrate your iTunes library to Spotify All you need to do is following:
  1. Open your iTunes
  2. File > Library > Export Library
  3. Upload the exported "Library.xml" file to Im Movin' App
